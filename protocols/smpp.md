# Encoding & Languages

|     |Data Coding   |
|-------|-----------|
| 0x00  | GSM 7 bit encoding  |
| 0x01  | US-ASCII  |
|0x03   | ISO8859-1 (Latin-1), delivered as GSM-7 |
|0x04   | Binary  |
|0x08   | UCS-2 / UTF-16BE  |
